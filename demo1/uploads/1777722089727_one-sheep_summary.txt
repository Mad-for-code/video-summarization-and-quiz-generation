Shila was a little wiggle worm. Her big brown eyes, usually so sparkly, were wide open, staring at the shadows dancing on her bedroom wall. Her favorite stuffed bunny, floppy, was tucked under her arm, but even floppy soft fur couldn't coax her to sleep. The sheep kept coming, each one doing something magical and wonderful. The colors began to swirl, the sound softened, and the sheep started to fade into a gentle, blurry haze.
